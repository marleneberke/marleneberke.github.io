Marlene D. Berke

Hi! I'm a fifth-year PhD student in the Computational Social Cognition Lab at Yale University advised by Dr. Julian Jara-Ettinger. I study how we think about ourselves and others thinking. I use a combination of behavioral experiments and computational modeling.

Before Yale, I was a College Scholar at Cornell University. I was lucky enough to spend a summer in Hakwan Lau's lab at UCLA.
